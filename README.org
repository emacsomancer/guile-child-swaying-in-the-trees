The idea of using what I've come to think of as the "Guile Chil(d)", the child in a gnu costume drawn in a series of illustrations in 2015 by Luis Felipe López Acevedo[0] and used on the front-page of the Guile website[1], in combination with the Sway logo, swinging on one of the branches of the Sway tree (making it sway?) like Max in his wolf costume in that one illustration[2] in Maurice Sendak's /Where the Wild Things Are/[3] (which I assume may have been one of the influences on López Acevedo's Guile Chil(d) illustrations).
